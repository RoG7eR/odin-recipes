Garlic Naan

Home

Description

Garlic Naan bread is Indian leavened flatbread made with all-purpose flour and spiced with garlic. At restaurants, plain naan bread is usually brushed with garlic butter and served.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour - 2 ½ cups
  • Yeast - 1 teaspoon (instant or active dry yeast)
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoon (to activate the yeast)
  • Warm water - ¼ cup (to activate the yeast)
  • Plain yogurt - ¼ cup
  • Milk - ¼ cup (warm, for soft dough)
  • Oil or melted butter - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Garlic - 3-4 cloves (minced)
  • Butter - 3-4 tablespoons (melted)
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) - 2 tablespoons (chopped)

Steps:

  1. Activate the Yeast
  2. Make the Dough
  3. Prepare Garlic Butter Topping
  4. Shape the Naan
  5. Cook the Naan
  6. On a Skillet (Tawa)
  7. Serve
