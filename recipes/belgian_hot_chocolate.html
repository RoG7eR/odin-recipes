Description

A simply delicious European-style Belgian Hot Chocolate recipe from Wittamer, in Brussels, Belgium.

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate (high-quality, 60-70% cocoa) - 100 grams (about 3.5 ounces), finely chopped
  • Whole milk - 2 cups (500 ml)
  • Heavy cream - ¼ cup (optional, for extra richness)
  • Sugar - 1-2 tablespoons (adjust to taste)
  • Vanilla extract - ½ teaspoon (optional)
  • Pinch of salt - to enhance the chocolate flavor
