Chana Masala

Home

Description

Chana masala (also chole masala, or chole) is a chickpea curry originating in the Indian subcontinent. It is a staple dish in North Indian cuisine. It is often eaten with a deep-fried bread called bhatura.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (Chana) - 1 ½ cups cooked (or 1 can, drained and rinsed)
  • Onion - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, finely chopped or pureed
  • Ginger - 1-inch piece, minced or grated
  • Garlic - 3-4 cloves, minced or grated
  • Green chilies - 1-2, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • Cooking oil or ghee - 2-3 tablespoons

Steps:

  1. Cook the Chickpeas
  2. Prep Ingredients
  3. Heat Oil
  4. Add Whole Spices
  5. Sauté Onions
  6. Add Ginger, Garlic, and Chilies
  7. Cook Tomatoes
  8. Add Ground Spices
  9. Add Chickpeas
  10. Add Water
  11. Finish with Garam Masala and Tang
  12. Serve
